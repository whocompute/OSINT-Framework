Notes

This is a slightly more up-to-date version of the original OSINT Framework.

OSINT framework focused on gathering information from free tools or resources. The intention is to help people find free OSINT resources. Some of the sites included might require registration or offer more data for $$$, but you should be able to get at least a portion of the available information for no cost.

USE THESE LINKS AT YOUR OWN RISK! I am not responsible for the content or practices of the websites linked here. I will do my best to check the links that are added here from when I forked the project, but I have not even seen most of the links that were added before that. I cannot guarantee the legitimacy of the data you get from any of these websites. Use at your own risk.
